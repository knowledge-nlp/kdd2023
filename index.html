Welcome to the 2nd Workshop on Knowledge Augmented Methods for NLP (KnowledgeNLP-KDD'23)!

Introduction

Language models are being developed and deployed in many applications, “small”-scale and large-scale, generic and specialized, text-only and multimodal, etc. Meanwhile, the missingness of important knowledge causes limitations and safety challenges. The knowledge includes common sense, world facts, domain expertise, and especially the unique patterns that need to be discovered from big data applications. Training and inference processes of the language models can be and should be augmented with the knowledge. The first KnowledgeNLP workshop at AAAI 2023 successfully attracted the attention of scientists and engineers on knowledge augmentation towards higher language intelligence. This workshop at KDD 2023 offers a broad platform for idea sharing and discussion on a variety of topics, such as (1) the synergy between knowledge and language model, (2) scalable architectures that integrate NLP, knowledge graph, and graph learning technologies, (3) KnowledgeNLP for e-commerce, education, healthcare, etc., and (4) human factors and social good in KnowledgeNLP.
